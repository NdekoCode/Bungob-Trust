Maize/Bean farm

The western highlands of Cameroon produce over 55% of the white and black beans sold in the central Africa sub region. Maize and bean farms will raise group and household income as well as household food needs. Your gift will provide seeds, tools, training and preservation post-harvest for groups of 20 people each

Plantain/Cocoyam

This gift will provide all necessary inputs to groups of 15 people Plantain and cocoyam are among the staple food in Momo. But these are becoming more scarce and costly. Large plantations to grow these crops are recommended to meet very high local demand. Though they take longer to start generating income (over a year), the returns are relatively higher.

Motorised crop transporter

Transportation from farms or storage facility to the market is a major challenge in rural Momo Your gift will buy a motor bike and a locally built trailer that will ease transport of tools, materials and harvest.

Soil ploughing equipment

Growing food in very hard soil or digging irrigation canals to get water to crops is back breaking work and virtually impossible without the right tools for the job Your gift will provide farmers with the facility to lessen their suffering. Many people are scared from farming because of the difficulty with tilling the soil. With this tool and similar others, it is certain that many groups will happily engage in farming.
