OUR VISION

An empowered community in which Poverty and suffering, as a result of inadequate access to basic human needs, are concerns of the past.

OUR MISSION

To develop the capacity of individuals and communities to overcome poverty by supporting and empowering them to identify, design, plan, implement and sustainably maintain the development of livelihood and income generating activities, thereby promoting opportunities for community self-reliance and growth.

OUR VALUES

Transparency - Respect - Inclusiveness - Progress - Accountability

  • Transparency
  • Respect
  • Progress
  • Accountability

Our current projects include:

Support for the most disadvantaged
Promotion of community peace
Promote actions that generate journals
Fight poverty
Environmental protection.
Bongob TrustBuilding Tomorrow Today

Bongob Trust is about developing a model for people and communities to build peace and overcome poverty especially in rural Africa while adapting to an evolving climate and world order.
