Our main goal

Our main goal is resolve the agro pastoral problem in cameroon with the sisal plant

Bongob Trust is a not-for-profit, non-governmental organisation formed in 2011 by a small group of people with backgrounds in engineering, agriculture, economic development and social work. The people involved have strong family links to the area and have supported individuals and other projects from the area in the past.

Read about some of our Members

Our in-depth knowledge of the challenges, the socio-cultural strata and the economic potentials within Momo area have motivated us to conceive a structure capable of blending individual and collective efforts into one unifying and compelling force capable of generating adequate confidence for communities to build their resilience to withstand the shocks of the future. A future in which women, men and children enjoy equal opportunities and are self-sufficient with respect to access to food, water, shelter, education and health care in dignity.

We engage with community members with the aim of supporting them improve their living standards through sustainable economic development within their communities as the first principle. Thus empowered, they can in turn be the driving force behind the development efforts of their villages. We prioritise the use of local resources and also plan to have projects self-financing within 5 years.

Social and environmental concerns take priority in all our operations as we believe economics alone will not secure sustainability.

Our work focuses on three strategic themes, through which we strive to help develop the capacity of marginalised, underserved and disadvantaged communities so that they are more resilient, in particular to economic and climate change shocks. The themes include:

  • Social Justice:

    through which we engage communities in peace building, promote gender equality, encourage community support to the most vulnerable (orphans, disabled and the elderly)

  • Health and Wellness

    through which we strive to support communities step up their life expectancy by improving access to safe water and sanitation and curving the incidence of HIV/Aids

  • Wealth Creation

    through provision of micro loans and the promotion of entrepreneurship

“ If justice & peace reign - if we are well & healthy - then we can create wealth ”

We plan to gradually fade out of the area (exit strategy) as empowered communities visibly and decisively build up their resilience into their culture and tradition. Our work starts in Momo division and in some years to come, we expect to repeat successful results of our action in other divisions of the Region and eventually beyond.

OUR VISION

An empowered community in which Poverty and suffering, as a result of inadequate access to basic human needs, are concerns of the past.

OUR MISSION

To develop the capacity of individuals and communities to overcome poverty by supporting and empowering them to identify, design, plan, implement and sustainably maintain the development of livelihood and income generating activities, thereby promoting opportunities for community self-reliance and growth.
