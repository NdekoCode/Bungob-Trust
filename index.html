Developing a model that promotes peace and overcomes poverty

We believe that in a majority of poor communities – scarcity occurs in the midst of local and unexplored abundance. That is why our approach to combat poverty largely rests on actions that tend to develop and promote growth from locally available resources. Poverty manifests itself in the same way across most of Africa.

Build a reproducible model that blends with local realities.

We work to build an environmentally-friendly model that is: replicable , scalable and sustainable. One that local communities can quickly take ownership of.

Thus, we will be able to solve certain problems visible in our communities such as the destruction of crops by forest fires and the invasion of animals (cows),the cry of farmers following the anguish of their harvests thus restoring social cohesion remains which today in some communities remains a treasure of the past.

Our current projects include:

Support for the most disadvantaged
Promotion of community peace
Promote actions that generate journals
Fight poverty
Environmental protection.
